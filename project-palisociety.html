Palsociety

Role: Visual & Interactive Designer

This is a hotel and brand website which I designed at spherical for Palisociety. Palisociety is a hotel group known for being the pioneer of unfussy down-to-earth "luxury" and is now expanding into top markets such as Seattle, Portland and San Francisco. The project involved a refresh to their existing brand and a redesign of multiple sites that would work well in their new design system. I collaborated directly with the creative director, brand lead and client throughout this design overhaul.
